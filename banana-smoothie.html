Peanut butter banana smoothie

Ingredients

  • 1 frozen sliced banana
  • 2 tablespoons of peanut butter
  • 1 tablespoon of brown sugar
  • 1/4 tablespoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of milk

Steps

  1. Add the ingredients to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth. You can add more milk to make it smoother. If too thin, add more banana. Taste and adjust.
