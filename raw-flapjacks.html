Raw vegan flapjacks

Ingredients

  • 100g unsalted peacans
  • 100g unsalted hazelnuts
  • 20g mixed seeds, such as linseeds or chia
  • 180g Medjool dates
  • 100g mixed dried berries, such as blueberries or cranberries.Â´
  • 200g porridge oats
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1-2 tablespoons maple syrup

Steps

  1. Place the pecans, hazelnuts, seeds and destoned dates and berries in a food processor and blitz until nicely chopped together.
  2. Add oats, oil and maple syrup and pulse until combined, although with texture.
  3. Crunch and squash into a rough sausage then divide into 24 flapjacks.
  4. Pat and push the portions firmly into a pastry cutter. As you push it down, remove the cutter to give you a round flapjack snack.
  5. Place the snacks in an airtight container, they will keep for up to two weeks. You can bag and freeze for a later date.
